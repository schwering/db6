About

Dingsbums is a web search engine for news-sites and blogs. It crawls the web, searches for RSS and Atom feeds, subscribes and then supervises them.

The software behind Dingsbums is written from scratch. The part that I'm interested most in is the storage engine (DBMS). All software is written in Ada. The operating system is Linux using XFS.

The hardware is currently two normal computers; they cost about 250 € a piece. The internet connection speed is, depending on where the computers are, fast or slow. Very fast. Or very slow.

My name is Christoph Schwering. I'm studying Computer Science at RWTH Aachen University. I'm writing Dingsbums because I'd like to play around with "large" amounts of data. My e-mail address is schwering (ät) gmail.com.
